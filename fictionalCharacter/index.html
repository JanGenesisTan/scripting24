About Laharl

Laharl is known for his loud and annoying laughter. Although he is a half demon, he doesnt actually do anything evil or immoral aside from beating up monsters or other demons who are rebelling or are in his way. His famous line is "Etna's vassals are my vassals, and my vassals do what I say! I shall burn a true vision of horror into that empty head of yours!"

Lahar's Skills

Laharl has 3 signature skills called the Meteor Impact, Blazing Knuckle and Overlord’s Wrath which are all very powerful abilities that ironically can devastate the planet that he is living on, but he tones it down a bit to not destroy his planet.

  1. Meteor Impact
  2. Blazing Knuckle
  3. Overlord’s Wrath

Laharl's Companions

Laharl has 3 strong companions that accompany him in his journey through the Netherworld.

  1. Etna, a manipulative demon with a devious personality.
  2. Flonne, a mischievous assassin, who is ironically an angel.
  3. The Prinnies, who are a reincarnation of sinful human souls that serve Laharl, (but mostly Etna).

The Netherworld

The Netherworld is a large and vast place where different planets are ruled by Demons with the most powerful being called the Overlord. One of the planets in the Netherworld is where Laharl is currently living at, and he is aiming to be the Overlord of that planet.
