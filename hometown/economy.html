ECONOMY

Marikina city is one of the wealthiest cities in the Philippines in terms of economy because the people and government are well disciplined and are focused for the betterment of the city. The shoe industry is the pride and joy of the city because it is one of the primary reasons for growth of its economy. There is also an increase of employment in the city because of the new buildings that were built, and new businesses being established in the city.

The biggest sector for employment in the city is shoe manufacturing because there are a lot of shoe factories around the city. The food districts and shopping malls in the city are usually busy with people. The green parks and river also have a lot of people who just wants to relax and have a picnic with their friends or family. Marikina is a very livable and safe city where you will have many ways for transportation and a lot of things to do for excitement.
