GEOGRAPHY

Marikina city is located in Luzon and is one of the well-known cities in the province of Metro manila, Philippines. Marikina has a geography of a valley which is surrounded by mountain ranges and is sliced by a river. The famous river that provides water to the city for industrial purposes is called the Marikina River and is one also one of the tourist attraction in the city.

Marikina is a beautiful city where nature and urban life blends well together. There are a lot of green parks and wide-open spaces for relaxation. The city also has a lot of buildings, restaurants and a lot of small businesses for people to explore. The feeling of having both nature and urban life all packaged in one city is wonderful. Transportation in the city is not a problem because there are a lot of vehicles, but for people who want to walk, the city is pedestrian-friendly and is also safe because a lot of police patrols around the city. There are also ancestral houses and old buildings that are still preserved until today that attracts a lot of people.
