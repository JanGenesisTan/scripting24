MARIKINA CITY

Marikina is a highly urbanized city in the National Capital Region of the Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 456,159 people. It is also one of the cleanest cities in the Philippines.

Marikina is also known as the Shoe Capital of the Philippines because it is well known for the manufacturing and selling of shoes. Despite the large number of people living here, the crime rate is low because of the responsive local government. We also have beautiful natural attractions and quality food that attracts a lot of people.
