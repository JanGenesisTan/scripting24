PEOPLE

The people in Marikina city are very friendly. The people living here are dedicated to the cleanliness of the environment because a lot of police patrols the city to make sure that rules are being followed for the betterment of the city. People in Marikina treat each other with respect and are very courteous to one another. A lot of people living here are also helpful and welcoming. It makes you feel at home even if you are just a visitor.

The city has a lot of green parks, and every street corner has garbage cans for proper disposal of trash. People in the city can usually be found in the park as lifestyle activities like walking, jogging, and bicycling are very common for people to do outdoors. There are also small businesses like restaurants owned by residents in the city. People in this city love to support small businesses and do volunteer work to maintain the quality of the city. This city is one of the best places to either live or visit because it can be an exciting experience for a lot of people as it offers a lot of different things.
