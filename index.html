ABOUT

ME

Jan Genesis Tan is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production Program focused on web development at Lethbridge Polytechnic. He is deeply fascinated in the creation of websites and UI/UX designs.


He was born and raised in Pasay city, Philippines. He then moved to Marikina city together with his parents when he entered High School and lived there until he grew up.


He loves to go out with his friends and do outdoor activities like mountain hiking and running marathons. In his personal free time, he loves to watch horror movies like the Insidious series and play turn-based strategy games like Persona 3 Reload as this is a form of relaxation for him.


He is more of an outdoor person and has travelled to a lot of places because he believes living life to the fullest can be experienced more in traveling and experiencing the outside world.

MY PASSION

TRAVELING

Genesis loves to travel to different places because it opens a person's eyes to things. One of the main benefits of traveling is collecting new experiences.

It can be physical, emotional, or psychological. For example, trying local cuisines and experiencing other countries' cultures can be a very fun and fascinating experience.
